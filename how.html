How to Play

To play the game select a color piece by clicking one of the circles in the top. Then click the position in the board you want to place that piece. You can only play one row at a time starting from the top. Your goal is to match the random pattern set up by the computer. After you set up your row, click the 'answer' cell in that row to get feedback. A red dot means a piece in the right spot, a white (hollow) dot means a correct colored piece but in the wrong location.

For more details see the Mastermind Wikipedia Page
